**Stay Safe Online – Student Checklist**

☐ Use strong, unique passwords for every account (mix of letters, numbers, symbols; avoid names & birthdays).
☐ Enable 2-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
☐ Never share OTPs or verification codes with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank or college. (UPI/financial safety)
☐ Double-check UPI requests before approving—“Collect Requests” can be scams; only pay people you trust. (UPI/financial safety)
☐ Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks like banking, logging into email, or making payments.
☐ Verify links before clicking—watch for fake job offers, internship links, or “urgent” messages from unknown numbers.
☐ Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store/App Store; avoid APKs from random links.
☐ Check privacy settings on social media—limit who can see your posts, phone number, and location.
☐ Log out from shared devices (college labs, cyber cafes) and don’t save passwords on them.
☐ Keep your devices updated (OS + apps) to protect against security vulnerabilities.

**If Something Goes Wrong**
☐ Report immediately on: https://cybercrime.gov.in
☐ Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (especially for financial fraud—act fast to try and stop the transaction)
