# Digital_Literacy
🌟 Overview

Welcome to my Digital Literacy Portfolio, a curated collection of tasks that demonstrate my ability to navigate, create, and communicate effectively in the digital world.

This portfolio reflects not only my technical skills but also my awareness of online safety, professional identity, and responsible digital behavior—all essential in today’s academic and professional environments.
