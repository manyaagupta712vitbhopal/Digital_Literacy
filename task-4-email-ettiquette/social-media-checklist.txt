✔️ Do’s
☐ Think before you post—assume anything online is permanent
☐ Keep your profiles private or control who can view your content
☐ Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news)
☐ Be respectful and mindful in comments and messages
☐ Use strong passwords and enable 2-factor authentication

❌ Don’ts
☐ Don’t share personal details like phone number, address, or OTPs
☐ Don’t post content that could harm your reputation (now or future)
☐ Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or hate speech
☐ Don’t accept friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts
☐ Don’t click on suspicious links or giveaways that seem too good to be true
